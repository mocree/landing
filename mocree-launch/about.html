What is Mocree?

Mocree is more than just another web and mobile app, it's a mindset and a movement. Our mission is simple... we want to help musicians everywhere GATHER, CONNECT, COLLABORATE and SHARE as effectively as possible. We value the idea of community, true and meaningful connections, productivity, and co-creation with others.

Our goal is not to replace traditional methods of music collaboration and creation but it's to assist and improve upon it. Mocree is the music collaboration tool for all musicians and those involved in the music creation process. If you believe in what we stand for, subscribe and follow us on social media!
